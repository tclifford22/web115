The Lone Mercenary

The second objective is to start shading in your values. There are a few ways to do this however I always chose to start in black and white. This is a really good technique when focusing on values which will elimate the dual stress of colors. When you focus on both of these at the same time you can often mess both of them up while trying to focus on and juggle to much at once. This example is from my Lone Mercenary.

Throwing Knives

When starting in black and white I tend to work faster and more effiecently, only worrying about basic lighting and values. As you can see hear in my Throwing Knives piece I am sticking to one light source and making sure everything is mattching while focusing on deep shadows and Highlights, yet not forgetting about the still important middtones.
