The Lone Mercenary

In the beginning there are two types of ways to start off Digital Illustrating. The first way is to draw with lines and rough out how your character or object is going to look, then refining it over and over again using layers until you have the look you want. This piece is an example of me doing that in the early stages of my Lone Mercenary piece.

Throwing Knives

The second option is to draw in blocks of shapes using silhouettes. This can be less time consuming and easier to roughout and shape your correct proportions, however I have found through experience, that becuase this is not how I orginally learned as a kid it takes time to deveop and may take longer in the beginning. This example is from my most recent prop/weapon design, Throwing Knives.
